Hotel Accomodations

From the ground floor, a handsome wooden staircase leads up to the 12 guest rooms.

The rooms have brass numbers on the doors and contain comfortable beds, polished oak furniture, a fireplace and a mirror that talks in a wheezy voice.

The famous Harry Potter once stayed at the hotel for several weeks in August 1993. If our hotel is good enough for Mr. Potter, you can be sure it's good enough for you.

Children and pets are welcome. Optional luggage delivery and storage.

Please note: NO products from Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes are allowed on the premises.
